Court of the Cold Prince

A fey retinue tied to winter and frost. Their sign is ice that does not thaw.

The Cold Prince appears on the murals of Droomen Knoll as a frost-clad knight beside Prince Mallowheart. The two are facing away from each other, so it's unclear whether this records an old alliance or something more fractious.

What the party knows of the Court's present doings comes from only two sources: the murals, and what little Thinwhistle said on his way out of the dungeon.
