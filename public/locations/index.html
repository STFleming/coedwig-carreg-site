  • Featureless Tower

    A slender, crooked, windowless tower of stone at the river bank. It impossibly ascends hundreds of yards into the sky, and yet is not visible from a distance. A yawning arch at ground level provides entry.

  • River Hameth

    The river the party came in on, aboard the Greasy Eel under Goodjen Filch . Upstream: the Falls of Nyf . Above them, the river broadens past The Shantywood Isles toward Dreg . The lower stretch is gated by House Mulbrek at the base of the Falls; they take a toll.

  • The Falls of Nyf

    The Hameth takes a thunderous drop at the legendary Falls of Nyf, where surging white waters tumble down a 100' precipice. A majestic and spectacular sight. It also is a wondrous ship conveyor and ancient magic is able to transport boats up and down the falls.

  • The Shantywood Isles

    A cluster of isles on the upper River Hameth , not far from Dreg . Goodjen Filch recommends them over Dreg for a night's rest. The party hasn't been yet.
