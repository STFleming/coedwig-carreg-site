River Hameth

The river the party came in on, aboard the Greasy Eel under Goodjen Filch.

Upstream: the Falls of Nyf. Above them, the river broadens past The Shantywood Isles toward Dreg. The lower stretch is gated by House Mulbrek at the base of the Falls; they take a toll.
