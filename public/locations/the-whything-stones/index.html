The Whything Stones

A ring of trilithons, a few miles south-west of Droomen Knoll. The party hasn't been. What they've heard:

  • The orange gem from the dead drune at the knoll gave a vision on touch: a twitching, twine-coated stag corpse surrounded by trilithon stones, with the warning Ill omens at the Whything Stones. Winter cometh.
  • In Clement Dunwallow's dream, a thousand years gone, Princess Snowfall-at-dusk and Lady-heart-torn-in-tatters spoke the stones' name alongside Sir Chyde.
  • Thinwhistle said the Cold Prince's daughter used to meet her kin there. Whether that's still true, nobody knows.
