NPCs

  • Brother Hogbeard

    Leader of a band of zealots in Dreg who hand out their own rough justice now that proper law has ebbed away. The party hasn't met him. One of his prisoners, a young man they rescued at Droomen Knoll , was caught for a petty theft, beaten, and only got free by punching his way out.

    Captain of the Greasy Eel , the river barge that brought the party up the River Hameth to the foot of the Falls of Nyf . Plain-spoken, no time for pretensions.     A corrupted breggle, about seven feet tall, with gnarled horns and a heavy greasy fur cloak. His eyes glowed; he breathed the fumes of noxious yellow candles that burned in a ring around him.   • Lady-heart-torn-in-tatters

    Mortal daughter of Prince Mallowheart . The party knows her elven name: Lady-heart-torn-in-tatters . In Clement Dunwallow 's dream she appeared with a brilliant diamond set in her brow, and it is by that sign that Clement first knew her.   • Lord Slumberbone

    A portly fey noble, named on one of the frost-chamber murals at Droomen Knoll . Nothing else is known.

  • Prince Mallowheart

    A fey prince of the Wood: slender, cruel. Clement knows him from his dreams, and his face is on a mural in the corridor beneath Droomen Knoll , under the inscription: On the slow creep of eons, Mallowheart's mercy may shine. His mortal daughter is Lady-heart-torn-in-tatters , depicted in the frost-chamber beside him…

  • Princess Snowfall-at-dusk

    Fey daughter of The Cold Prince . A thousand years ago, in Clement Dunwallow 's dream, she walked the halls of Droomen Knoll with Lady-heart-torn-in-tatters .   • Rescued Boy

    A young man the party found bound and twitching in Grognot 's chamber under Droomen Knoll , where Grognot had been keeping him. Clement walked him out of the dungeon while the others finished up.   • Sir Chyde

    A knight whose name came up in Clement Dunwallow 's dream, a thousand years past, in passing talk between Princess Snowfall-at-dusk and Lady-heart-torn-in-tatters . Mentioned alongside The Whything Stones . Nothing else is known.
