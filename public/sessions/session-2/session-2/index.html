session_2

“Get Grognot!” the breggle screams as she emerges from the top right tower brandishing a set of pots and pans. Hearing this Barney decides to flee back to the main group. Swinging his mace Fennic drops the first breggle to the ground whilst around him arrows fly and swords slash against armour. Barney is bludgeoned from behind from a pursuing breggle as to everyone's surprise the winged creatures fly over at everyone and start attacking indiscriminately. Obolm falls to an attack whilst the breggles and flying creatures are slain. As the combat comes to an end we pull Barney and Obolm to their feet and proceed into the courtyard, hearing pan pipes echo on the wind. The fire in the middle shows the breggle have been hunting and eating Woodgrue. As a group they head down the stairs, following the sound of the music.

Down on the next level we see crushed and killed breggle lying limply on the damp flagstones, it’s the one from upstairs with the two pans. In the corner there is a cockerell type creature with two heads, asleep and tied to door. Fennic remains on the stairs so as to not make a sound while the gang sneaks around the corner into the corridor. They see a ornate sundial, with a glowing gnomen. Behind him there is an elven figure inscribed on the wall with the words “on the slow creep of eons mallowhearts mercy may shine” upon it. Clement recognises the elf from his dreams, he is Prince Mallowheart. In one of the rooms there are loose bones and scratching on the floor. Ahead they can hear the pan pipes playing. Now joined by Fennic, they form up at the door and open it to reveal a bar, prison cell, inhabited by a ragged elven man with wild tangledhair, he is playing a set of pan pipes carved from rat bones. “Oh my goodness! Hello! Other people not breggle fiends!” he scans across us and stops on Clement and recognises him. Clement does not. The elf names himself as ThinWhistle and says he has been wrongly imprisoned….
