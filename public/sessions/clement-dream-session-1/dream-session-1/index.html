dream_session_1

A dream of Droomen Knoll a thousand years ago. Clement alone; the others are not here.

He wakes in a cold cell, about fifteen feet square, with a barred iron door. The walls are sheathed in ice, but the place is not cold to the touch.

Across from him sits a drune in dark robes, marked with occult sigils. Clement never learns his name. The drune is scraping at the floor, looking for something; what, and why, the dream does not say.

Two women's voices come up the corridor. Princess Snowfall-at-dusk and Lady-heart-torn-in-tatters stop at the bars. They did not come to free him, but Clement talks them into it. He can no longer remember exactly what he said. They turn the key and let him out.

Walking with them, he catches two names in their talk: The Whything Stones and Sir Chyde.

Further on, an alchemist stands over a stone basin ringed with stag-skulls, a chalice already half-risen in his hand. The alchemist takes Clement for a willing donor come to give mortal blood for the spell, and does not realise he has escaped from the cell. Clement plays along long enough to grab the chalice, then runs.

Out of the dungeon and into the cold. He hides the chalice and goes to ground.

Mallowheart's frost-clad retinue catches him in the end. They bring him before Prince Mallowheart. An antler wraith stands at the prince's shoulder. Clement fights well. the lady tries to spare him with a Vapours of Dream from across the hall, but it isn't enough. The wraith kills Clement. The spell kills her.

And Clement wakes.

Remembered by Clement Dunwallow. First glimpsed on the eve of the company's journey up the River Hameth.
