Session 1 : Up the Falls of Nyf

Journeying into Dolmenwood up the Nyf Falls

Weather - Hot and Humid

We are journeying on the Greasy Eel, captained by Goodjen Filch up river Hameth, we hear a loud noise drowning everything else on the boat. Ahead we see a huge waterfall, causing all the noise. At the foot of the falls there is a fortified wooden gate which the captain navigates us over to. There are guards from House Mulbrek coming out of the gatehouse who ask us to pay a toll. Barney McGrue steps forward to persuade them with a song (Take on me) to let us through. The love it and let us through, opening the gate and allowing us to access the Falls of Nyf. Goodjen becomes enamoured with Barney McGrue and complains about how shitty the Town Dreg has become, he recommends we stay at the shanty wood isles nearby. As we progress we suddenly see a featureless tower, off to the shore, it’s inhabited by the creator of the Nyf falls (a mad wizard). We also see a ruin on a hill off to the East that Clement recognises from a dream (it was covered in ice, as a group we decide to investigate. As we disembark we can hear faint music, as we approach the knoll this gets louder and we start to hear drums and pan pipes. We approach a ruined tower atop the knoll (Droomen Knoll), we see a figure at the top of the tower who hasn’t noticed us yet. Obolm and Ghrend stealth their way up towards the tower and see 3 goatlike figures dancing around a fire, they are preparing food. We see 3 breggles that look gnarled and scary looking compared to a normal respectable Breggle. Half the group head to the right and explore a ruined tower while Fennic and Clement hold the middle. In the ruined tower they find a small 2 ft hole that drops down into a 10ft drop. The breggle see the group and start to prepare to attack. Fennic steps up in braces himself in the archway as the fight begins. Maces are swung and spears are stabbed. Emerging from the hole we now see two winged creatures fly out, the Breggles don’t seem to be friendly to them.
